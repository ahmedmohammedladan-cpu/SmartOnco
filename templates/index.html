About SmartOnco

SmartOnco is an AI-powered Cancer Diagnosis System developed to assist in detecting different types of cancer such as Breast, Lung, and Prostate cancer using advanced machine learning models. It helps medical practitioners and patients get early insights into possible risks through predictive analysis.

This project was built by Ahmed Mohammed Ladan, a Computer Science student at Gombe State University, as part of a research initiative to integrate Artificial Intelligence into healthcare systems for better diagnostic support.
